Our Story

Crafted With
Devotion & Skill

ASURA INK is Pattaya's premier destination for fine art tattooing. Our studio was built from an obsession with turning skin into breathtaking, permanent art — every piece as unique as the person who carries it.

Specializing in Realism, Black & Gray, and full Color tattooing, our award-winning team brings extraordinary technical mastery to every session. We are proud partners of Intenze Asia Black Label, using only the highest quality inks available.

Located at 373/10 Pattaya 3rd Road, we serve both local Thai and international clients who trust us with their most meaningful body art — and return again and again.

How It Works

Your Journey

From first message to finished masterpiece.

01

DM & Consult

Message us on Instagram or fill our booking form. Share your ideas, reference images, placement, and size.

02

Design & Deposit

We create a custom design based on your brief. A deposit secures your appointment slot.

03

Arrive at our studio. We review the design, adjust placement, and begin the tattooing process with full care.
